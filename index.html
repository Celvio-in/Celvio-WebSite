FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about working with Celvio.

What process nodes do you support?

+
We support a wide range of process nodes from mature nodes (180nm, 130nm, 65nm) to advanced FinFET nodes (28nm, 16nm, 7nm) across foundries including TSMC, GlobalFoundries, UMC, and Samsung.

How do you protect our IP and design files?

+
We sign mutual NDAs before any technical discussion begins. All design work happens in secure, isolated environments with access controls. Source files are transferred via encrypted channels and deleted from our systems upon project completion if required.

Do you work with international clients?

+
Yes — we actively serve clients in the United States, Europe (Germany, UK, Netherlands), and across Asia (India, Taiwan, Singapore, South Korea). We adjust meeting times to overlap with your business hours and communicate in English.

What is your typical project timeline?

+
Timelines vary by project complexity. Small blocks (LDO, comparator) typically take 4–8 weeks. Medium blocks (ADC, PLL, digital subsystem) take 8–16 weeks. Full-chip physical design engagements are scoped individually based on complexity and target node.

Can you work with our existing design team?

+
Absolutely. We integrate well as an extension of your in-house team — whether as a dedicated resource, a specialized sub-team, or a review and signoff partner. We are comfortable working within your existing flows and toolchains.

How do I get started?

+
Simply email us at hello@celvio.in with a brief description of your project — the design type, target process node, and rough timeline. We'll schedule a free 30-minute discovery call within 24 hours and follow up with a detailed proposal.
